Connecting LGBTQIA+ People Across Africa

Ubuntu is a digital space where LGBTQIA+ Africans can connect, share, and thrive. Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive online community for LGBTQIA+ individuals across the continent.

About the App

Ubuntu is a mobile application designed to foster a sense of community and belonging among LGBTQIA+ Africans. It provides a platform for individuals to connect with peers, access resources, and share their stories in a secure and supportive environment. The app is intended for LGBTQIA+ individuals living in Africa, as well as allies who wish to support the community.
